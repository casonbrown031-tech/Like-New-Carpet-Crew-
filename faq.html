Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about low-moisture carpet cleaning, dry times, pets, and more.

How long does it take for carpets to dry?

Most carpets dry in 1–2 hours depending on ventilation, humidity, and carpet type.

Is your cleaning safe for kids and pets?

Yes — once dry, carpets are safe for kids and pets. Our process avoids over-wetting.

Do you offer odor treatment for pet accidents?

Yes — we use targeted chemistry designed to help with pet urine and odor sources.

Is low-moisture cleaning as effective as steam cleaning?

We use mechanical agitation to scrub the dirt out of the fibers, giving you a deep clean that dries fast without soaking your carpets.

Will there be sticky residue left behind?

No — we neutralize and groom carpets so no detergent residue remains.

How far in advance should I book?

Fast response: same-day and emergency services are regularly available.
