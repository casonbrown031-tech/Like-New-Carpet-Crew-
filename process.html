How Our Low‑Moisture Process Works

We follow a clear, professional process so you know exactly what to expect from start to finish.

  1. 1. Inspection & Assessment

    We walk through your space, ask about problem areas, and inspect the carpet type, fiber, and existing stains.

    This helps us choose the right chemistry and method for your carpet.

  2. 2. Targeted Pre‑Treatment

    We apply professional cleaning solutions to traffic lanes, spots, and stains to loosen soils before agitation.

    Cleaning is chemistry — not flooding.

  3. 3. Mechanical Agitation

    Using specialized equipment, we gently agitate the carpet fibers to lift dirt and suspend soil for removal.

    This boosts cleaning power without soaking the carpet.

  4. 4. Low‑Moisture Extraction

    We extract the loosened soil and moisture using low‑moisture methods that avoid oversaturation.

    Deep cleaning without gallons of leftover water.

  5. 5. Neutralization & Grooming

    We neutralize the carpet to remove any sticky residue, then groom fibers so they dry soft and even.

    The result: clean, soft carpets with fast drying.
