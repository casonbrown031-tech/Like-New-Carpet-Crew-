How Our Low‑Moisture Process Works

We follow a clear, science‑based system that deep‑cleans without flooding your carpet.

  1. 1. Inspection & Assessment

    We walk through your space, identify problem areas, and determine the correct chemistry for your carpet type.

    Every carpet is different — we treat it that way.

  2. 2. Targeted Pre‑Treatment

    Professional solutions are applied to traffic lanes and stains to break down soil before agitation.

    Cleaning is chemistry — not soaking.

  3. 3. Mechanical Agitation

    We use CRB technology to lift dirt from deep within the fibers.

    This step restores carpet texture and boosts cleaning power.

  4. 4. Low‑Moisture Extraction

    We extract soil and moisture using controlled‑moisture methods that avoid oversaturation.

    Deep cleaning without gallons of leftover water.

  5. 5. Neutralization & Grooming

    We neutralize the carpet to remove residue, then groom fibers for a soft, even finish.

    The result: clean, fresh, fast‑drying carpets.
